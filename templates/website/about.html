Who we are

About Us

We are a travel agency headquartered in Cochin, Kerala, with branches across the state. Our Mattanur branch is conveniently located near the new Kannur International Airport.

Blue Lagoon Holiday Cruises Pvt. Ltd. delivers synergy in travel, tourism, and hospitality solutions at affordable costs. We offer a wide range of tour packages — both pre-designed and customized — tailored to your tastes and interests.

The destinations we curate will leave you with nostalgic memories. Houseboat cruises through palm-fringed backwaters, beach getaways, hill station resorts, and tea garden trails transform your journey into a close encounter with Mother Nature.

Operating since 2013 in Kerala — globally acclaimed as “God’s Own Country” — we confidently recommend destinations that ensure memorable, satisfying experiences. Our happy customers are our best testimony.

We are authorized agents of Lakshadweep Tourism under the Ministry of Tourism, Government of India. The “Samudram” Lakshadweep package offers economical on-board accommodation with day visits to three islands. This 5-day, 4-night tour is perfect for sea and beach lovers.

For groups of any size, we can arrange make-shift kitchens with cooking staff at visit locations.

Choose your tour theme and we’ll propose the most economical and satisfying route:

Houseboats | Hill Stations | Beaches | Traditional Villages | Cities | Historical Places | Temples | Mosques | Churches | Trekking | Tea Gardens | Wildlife | And More

Managed by courteous professionals with rich travel industry experience, Blue Lagoon ensures all your tour and travel needs are met — leaving you with happy, enduring memories.
