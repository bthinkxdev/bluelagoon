About Us

Blue Lagoon Holiday Cruises Pvt Ltd offers a multi-ranged variety of cruise options and on shore packages. We also help you locate mystical spots in South India for a tour. You can choose from hill stations, beach resorts, trekking routes, historical cities and modern metropolises for your visit.

We provide pre-designed and custom made tour packages Kerala, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka and Goa. Leave all your travel requirements to us – we cater to a wide number of budgets and enquiries.

We arrange ticketing, airport and ship terminal transfers, taxis and other facilities for travellers. Our cruises take you to Lakshadweep, Andaman and Nicobar Islands, Goa, Mumbai, Singapore and Malaysia. While on the islands here you can go for on-shore activities, diving and boating.
