Wayanad is located at the Western Ghats of Kerala. It is a green paradise nestled in the mountain region. The entire city is with enchanting and hypnotizing culture and history. Ideal time to visit the place is between September and March.

Edakkal Caves

One of the most ancient caves with carvings and paintings of Stone Age. The history of the caves can be traced over 5000 years and had been colonized at various stages in history.

Banasura Dam

One of the biggest earth dams in India, located at the north-west part of Wayanad, extended to a wide area with crystal clear waters throughout the area.

Pazhassi Raja Tomb

Located in Mananthavady in Wayanad, revealing the history of Pazhassi Raja who was the first warrior to oppose British rule.

Other attractions include Wayanad wildlife sanctuary, Kuruva Island, and Soochipara falls.
